All black attire is required for all guests. The only exceptions are mens dress shirts being white, and ladies have a select few color options for their shoes listed below


Gentlemen

  • Black suit or tuxedo

  • White dress shirt

  • Black necktie or bow-tie

  • Black dress shoe



Ladies

  • Black floor length gown or formal cocktail dress

  • Black, silver, or gold formal footwear

  • Silver or gold jewelry is allowed
